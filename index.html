Biography

Yanjun Wang is an associate professor of Civil Aviation College of Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), an excellent young backbone teacher of Jiangsu Province's Blue and Blue Project, a visiting scholar of the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics of Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), a member of the Expert Group of the National 14th Five-Year Plan for "Intelligent Civil Aviation", and an excellent young scholar of the first Aviation Society of China's Aviation Avionics and Air Traffic Control Sub-Section. He has won two best paper awards in FAA/EUROCONTROL ATC top international conferences, one first prize of technical invention in China Intelligent Transportation Association, one second prize of National Defense Science and Technology Progress Award, and one second prize of Civil Aviation Science and Technology Progress Award.His research areas are air transportation system optimization and human factors in aviation, and he has presided over 1 key project of the Civil Aviation Joint Fund of the National Natural Science Foundation of China, 2 upper-level projects, 1 joint fund project, and more than 10 scientific and technological projects of the Air Traffic Control Bureau of the Civil Aviation Administration of China (ATCB); he has also participated in 20 projects of the National Key Research and Development Program, and the Sino-European Scientific and Technological Cooperation Program; in the past five years, he has published 56 high-level academic papers, including 56 papers in the journals of TR A/C/E, Chinese Academy of Engineering, and Chinese Academy of Engineering. In the past five years, he has published 56 high-level academic papers, including more than 20 papers published in TR A/C/E, Engineering, the journal of the Chinese Academy of Engineering and other Chinese Academy of Sciences, and one academic monograph; his research results have been widely used in civil aviation related fields. He has served as a member of AV060 of the American Council for Transportation Research (ACTR) and a member of the organizing committee of ICRAT International Conference.
