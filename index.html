Biography

Dr. Xiaoliang FAN got a Ph.D degree in Computer Science from University Pierre and Marie Curie (now Sorbonne Université) in France in 2012. Now he works at Fujian Key Lab of Sensing and Computing for Smart Cities (Xiamen University) and ASC Lab as a Senior Research Specialist. His research interests include Spatio-temporal Data Mining, Privacy-preserving Computing & AI algorithms. He has published 60+ journal (IEEE TMC/TITS/TSC, etc.) and conference (AAAI, IJCAI, UbiComp, etc.) papers, and has obtained/ applied for 8/14 Chinese invention patents. His works are funded by NSFC and many industry collaborators. Dr. FAN is an IEEE Senior Member, and CCF (China Computer Federation) Senior Member.
